Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Applies to ibdpal.org only

1. Scope

This policy covers the public education and marketing website at ibdpal.org, operated by MediVue. It does not cover the IBDPal iOS app.

If you use the IBDPal iOS app, see the separate App Privacy Policy (accounts, health logs, and encryption).

2. What the website does not collect

You can browse articles, guides, and support pages without creating an account. The website is not a medical record system.

  • We do not collect personal health information (PHI) through ordinary website browsing.
  • We do not store meal logs, symptom diaries, medication schedules, or bowel records on the website.
  • We do not sell website visitor information.
  • Encryption of health data, app accounts, and related security claims apply to the iOS app, not to reading this website.

3. Voluntary contact

If you email us (for example support@ibdpal.org or privacy@ibdpal.org) or voluntarily submit a contact or newsletter form, we use that information only to respond or to send the updates you requested. You can unsubscribe from newsletters at any time.

4. Children

The website provides general education and does not target children for data collection. The IBDPal app has its own age and account rules in the App Privacy Policy.

5. Changes

We may update this Website Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.

6. Contact

  • Email: privacy@ibdpal.org
  • Address: MediVue, North Carolina, USA
