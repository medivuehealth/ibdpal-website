College With IBD: ImproveCareNow College & IBD Handbook

Posted on June 11, 2026 · ICN Resources

Content note: Reviewed for patient education accuracy against publicly available guidance from the Crohn's & Colitis Foundation and major IBD education sources. Last reviewed June 2026. Not individual medical advice.

Educational use only. IBDPal does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your gastroenterologist or IBD care team for personal decisions.

Transitioning to college with a chronic illness can feel overwhelming. The ImproveCareNow College & IBD Toolkit is a handbook co-produced by patients, parents, and clinicians to make the process less stressful.

Who it is for

High school students planning ahead, current college students managing flares away from home, and family members supporting the transition.

Topics inside the handbook

  • Choosing a school that fits your health and support needs
  • Preparing medically and practically before move-in
  • Surviving classes when fatigue or urgency spikes
  • Dorms, roommates, and shared bathrooms
  • Dining halls, meal plans, and safe food strategies
  • Disability services and accommodation letters

Get the toolkit

Download the College & IBD Toolkit and a shorter College & IBD Highlight from the ICN tools page linked below. Highlights are useful to share with school counselors or college health offices.
