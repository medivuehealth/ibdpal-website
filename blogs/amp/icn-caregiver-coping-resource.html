Caregiver Coping With Pediatric IBD: ImproveCareNow Family Resource

Posted on June 12, 2026 · ICN Resources

Content note: Reviewed for patient education accuracy against publicly available guidance from the Crohn's & Colitis Foundation and major IBD education sources. Last reviewed June 2026. Not individual medical advice.

Educational use only. IBDPal does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your gastroenterologist or IBD care team for personal decisions.

Parenting a child with Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis brings joy and real challenges. The ImproveCareNow Caregiver Coping Resource is a 160+ page co-produced guide from ICN's Parent/Family Advisory Council, social workers, psychologists, and A-LiNK Connections.

What caregivers will find

  • Navigating joys and challenges of parenting a child with a chronic condition
  • How stress impacts health for caregivers and children
  • Impact of chronic illness on siblings
  • Practical coping strategies from other caregivers and psychosocial professionals

Content is compiled by caregivers of children with IBD and autoimmune liver disease, alongside psychologists and a medical social worker who work with pediatric GI families.

Access the resource

Visit the ICN tools page for the full Caregiver Coping Resource and a one-page printable handout you can share at your care center.
