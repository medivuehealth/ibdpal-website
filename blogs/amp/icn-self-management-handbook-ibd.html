Content note: Educational content aligned with publicly available patient materials from the Crohn's & Colitis Foundation and other major IBD education sources. IBDPal is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Foundation. Last reviewed June 2026. Not individual medical advice.

Educational use only. IBDPal does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your gastroenterologist or IBD care team for personal decisions.

Learning to participate in your own IBD care is a long-term skill. The Living Well with Inflammatory Bowel Disease: A Self-Management Handbook is a comprehensive resource from pediatric IBD experts at Nationwide Children's Hospital and Cincinnati Children's, combined with practical tips from parents and kids living with Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis.

Who it helps

  • Families newly learning about IBD diagnoses
  • Young patients who want to understand tests, treatments, and daily management
  • Caregivers supporting active participation in clinic visits

What makes it useful

ImproveCareNow emphasizes cooperation between patients, families, and healthcare teams. The Handbook translates clinical knowledge into language families can use at home and in appointments.
