Trusted IBD Resources

No single website should be your only source for IBD education. Use IBDPal as a guide to practical tracking and patient-friendly pages, then verify decisions with your care team and reputable organizations.

Where each resource fits

  • IBDPal: free tracking app, practical guides, state support links, and curated patient education.
  • Crohn's & Colitis Foundation: national patient education, Help Center, chapters, research, and advocacy.
  • ImproveCareNow: pediatric IBD quality-improvement network and co-produced family resources.
  • GI Kids / NASPGHAN: pediatric GI education for families.
  • ACG and AGA: professional society information and clinical guideline context.
  • NIH / MedlinePlus: federal health education and condition summaries.

How to use resource pages safely

Look for publication dates, source citations, medical disclaimers, and whether content separates general education from personal medical advice. Bring questions to your GI team before changing medications, supplements, or flare plans.
