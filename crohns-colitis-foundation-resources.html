The Crohn's & Colitis Foundation is a major public source for IBD education, support programs, advocacy, and local chapters. IBDPal is independent and not affiliated with or endorsed by the Foundation.

IBD Help Center

The Foundation's IBD Help Center can help with education, support, and referrals to programs. It does not replace your gastroenterologist or emergency services.

  • Phone: 888-MY-GUT-PAIN (888-694-8872)
  • Use for general education, local resources, support groups, and program referrals.
  • For severe symptoms, call your GI team, urgent care, or emergency services.
