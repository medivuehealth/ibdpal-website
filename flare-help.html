Flares are frightening but manageable with a plan. These guides and articles cover early steps many patients discuss with their IBD team.

When to seek urgent care

Heavy bleeding, severe pain, fever, signs of dehydration, or rigid abdomen need prompt medical evaluation. Use your clinic's on-call instructions.
