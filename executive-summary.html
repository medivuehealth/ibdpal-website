Executive Summary
IBDPal is a comprehensive iOS application and web platform designed to revolutionize the management of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) and Crohn disease through advanced nutrition tracking, AI-powered flare risk assessment, and personalized patient care. Developed by MediVue, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in North Carolina, IBDPal addresses the critical gap in specialized healthcare technology for the 3.1 million Americans living with IBD.

Problem Statement
Current IBD management lacks personalized, data-driven approaches. Patients struggle with:
  • Nutritional Deficiencies: 60-80% of IBD patients experience malnutrition
  • Flare Prediction: No reliable early warning system for disease exacerbations
  • Fragmented Care: Disconnected tracking of symptoms, diet, and medications
  • Limited Research Integration: Lack of evidence-based nutrition guidelines
  • Isolation: Limited peer support and community connection
Solution & Technology
IBDPal provides a comprehensive ecosystem combining iOS mobile app, web platform, and AI-powered backend:
iOS Application
SwiftUI, Core Data, Charts, Combine
Backend Infrastructure
Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, JWT Security
AI & Analytics
Machine Learning, Trend Analysis, Flare Prediction
Core Features:
  • Daily Logging: Comprehensive tracking of meals, symptoms, medications, and bowel health
  • AI-Powered Flare Risk Assessment: 3-month trend analysis with early warning system
  • Research-Based Nutrition Analysis: FODMAP, IBD, and Crohn-specific benchmarks
  • Micronutrient Management: Deficiency identification and personalized recommendations
  • Community Platform: IBD Stories for peer support and success sharing
  • Smart Search & Discovery: AI-powered insights and trend visualization
