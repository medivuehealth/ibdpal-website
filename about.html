About IBDPal

Our mission: IBDPal helps people with Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis understand nutrition, flares, and daily management through free patient education and a tracking app from nonprofit MediVue.

Who we are

IBDPal is a program of MediVue, a North Carolina 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on IBD community education and self-management tools. We combine a free iOS app for food and symptom tracking with a growing library of articles, guides, and state support resources on ibdpal.org.

We are not a hospital, drug company, or substitute for your gastroenterologist. Everything on this site is educational. Clinical decisions belong with your care team.

What you will find here

  • 50+ in-depth articles on nutrition, treatment basics, wellness, and daily life
  • 24 step-by-step patient guides for flares, diet, travel, and more
  • 50 state support pages with chapters and helplines
  • 140+ total education pages including Spanish resources and topic hubs
  • Free IBDPal iOS app for logging meals, symptoms, and visit prep
